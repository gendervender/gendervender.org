Gender Vender

Shattering Gender Inequality Norms

A vending machine dedicated to educating others and uplifting women.

Through a survey we conducted, 95.5% of individuals believe gender inequality exists.

Our mission is to raise awareness on supporting local businesses run by women in the greater Seattle area, sparking dialogue on gender inequality, and enabling change.

How Gender Vender Works

01

Locate our pop-up vending machine on June 4th from 6pm to 9pm at the University of Washington Bothell Activities and Recreation Center 2nd Floor. We will have a limited stock of partnered items from three local-women run businesses.

02

All funds collected from the machine will be donated to the Refugee Women's Alliance, Women's Funding Alliance, and Seattle Now. If you'd like to donate online you can do so here.

03

We invite everyone to explore our website. We include a video series with our partnered businesses and an interactive timeline on historic women moments in history.
